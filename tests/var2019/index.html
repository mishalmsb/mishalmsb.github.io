Features Released

Exhibitions search Updated the way you can find touring exhibitions on our platform based on your budget, space or theme requirements

Requests An improved way for museums to request object loans and touring exhibitions, and smart matching between content and requests

Reports The world's first reports on the touring exhibition industry, using anonymised information from Vastari's museum users

Messaging Improved encrypted messaging service between users, providing a more streamlined experience and file management system

Collections search New featured search terms show museums what's popular on the platform, and allows Vastari to feature relevant content to the curators from the private collections on the platform
